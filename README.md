# 🍱 Rapid Rasoi

### Homemade Tiffin. Built Around Trust.

A **UI/UX case study and upcoming Flutter MVP** focused on solving the homemade food discovery problem for students and working professionals in tier-2 Indian cities.

Rapid Rasoi connects customers with trusted home kitchen providers through a subscription-based tiffin platform designed around transparency, consistency, and convenience.

## 🧩 The Problem

Every year, thousands of students and working professionals move to new cities for education or jobs.

One problem appears almost immediately:

> “Where can I find homemade food in a new city?”

Most people end up relying on:

* expensive restaurant food
* unhealthy daily takeout
* random tiffin contacts shared through WhatsApp
* inconsistent mess services

At the same time, many talented home cooks struggle to:

* find customers
* build credibility
* manage subscriptions professionally
* compete with larger food platforms

Rapid Rasoi is designed to bridge that gap.

---

## 💡 The Idea

Rapid Rasoi is a subscription-first homemade tiffin platform where users can:

* discover nearby home kitchens
* try meals before subscribing
* track deliveries transparently
* manage flexible meal plans

At the same time, home kitchen providers get tools to:

* showcase their food
* manage subscriptions
* build trust through reviews
* track orders and earnings

## ✨ Core Features

### 👨‍🍳 For Customers

#### 🔍 Discover Trusted Home Kitchens

Browse nearby kitchens with ratings, hygiene details, pricing, and food previews.

#### 🧪 Trial Meals Before Subscription

Users can order a single lunch or dinner before committing to a long-term plan.

#### 📅 Flexible Meal Plans

Choose between:

* weekly or monthly subscriptions
* lunch-only or dinner-only plans
* customizable meal schedules

#### 📊 Monthly Delivery Tracker

A visual calendar system to track:

* delivered meals
* missed deliveries
* partial deliveries

This improves transparency and accountability.

#### 🎁 Reward System

Earn reward points through subscriptions and redeem them for discounts or free meals.

---

### 🏠 For Home Kitchen Providers

#### 📣 Kitchen Profiles

Show menus, pricing, food photos, and customer reviews in one dedicated profile.

#### ✅ Build Customer Trust

Verified reviews and transparent ratings help cooks build credibility over time.

#### 📋 Subscription Management

Track:

* active subscribers
* daily deliveries
* monthly earnings
* order history

without relying on WhatsApp or manual records.

## 🧠 Planned Architecture

Rapid Rasoi is planned as a **dual-role application**:

### Customer Mode

Users can:

* discover kitchens
* subscribe to meal plans
* track deliveries
* manage subscriptions

### Kitchen Owner Mode

Kitchen providers can:

* manage orders
* update menus
* track payments
* monitor active subscribers

Both experiences will share a single Flutter codebase with role-based navigation and permissions.
